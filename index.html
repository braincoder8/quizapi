What is it all about?

The QuizAPI is a SaaS which lets you test your knowledge on a wide variety of technical topics. You can create your own Quiz or get a random set of questions for a specific topic including Linux, DevOps, BASH, PHP, and lots more. We offer an easy-to-use API which allows you to embed the quiz on your own website.
